I am a Ph. D. Candidate in the Discovery Analytics Center at Virginia Tech, under the advisory of Prof. Chang-Tien Lu. I worked on several research projects on urban perception with deep learning, traffic impact analysis for smart cities, and emerging technologies prediction. My research and teaching focus on Spatial Data Mining, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, GeoAI, Social Media Analysis, and Urban Computing.

I received my Master of Science degree in Computer Science from Virginia Tech in 2015. I studied Social Media Analysis and Spatial Data Mining. There I worked on Social Media-based Transportation-related Event Detection projects.

Before that, I hold a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering in the direction of Electronic Communication Engineering.
